BSharp Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

Developer

BSharp is developed by WELES.

Data collection

BSharp does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app contains no analytics, advertising, or crash reporting services.

Local storage

Data retrieved from mobireg.pl (grades, attendance, messages, etc.) is cached locally on your device to enable offline access. This data never leaves your device.

Credentials

Your login credentials are stored locally on your device and are sent only to mobireg.pl for authentication. They are never shared with any other party.

Third-party services

BSharp connects to mobireg.pl to retrieve your school data. Please refer to mobireg.pl for their privacy practices.

Optional translation

If you use the translation feature, text may be processed by:

  • Google ML Kit (on-device, no data sent to external servers)
  • DeepL API (only if you provide your own API key)

Changes to this policy

Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

Questions? Open an issue at github.com/brothware/bsharp.
